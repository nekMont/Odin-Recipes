In this recipe we will explain how you can cook a delicious alfredo for yourself.

Ingredients are below

  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • your choice of Pasta shape
  • Parmesiano Regiano

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Bring heavy cream to a simmer
  3. Shred Cheed into sauce
  4. pour 1 cup of heavy whipping cream into pan to cool sauce
